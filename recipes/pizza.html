Elise pizza Recipe

This is Elise's pizza recipe which I got from the internet

Ingredients

Dough

    1 1/2 cups (355 ml) warm water (105°F-115°F) 2 package (2 1/4 teaspoons) active dry yeast 3 3/4 cups (490 g) bread flour 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil (omit if cooking pizza in a wood-fired pizza oven) 2 teaspoons salt 1 teaspoon sugar
